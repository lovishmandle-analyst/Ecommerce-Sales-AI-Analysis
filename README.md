# Key Analytical Insights Generated (VIMP)
## Sales Performance Insights
Revenue concentration is driven by a limited number of high-performing products
Seasonal patterns directly influence sales volume and revenue spikes
Growth is uneven across categories, highlighting expansion and rationalization opportunities

## Customer Behavior Insights
A smaller segment of repeat customers contributes disproportionately to revenue
Purchasing behavior varies significantly by category, impacting pricing and bundling strategies

## Operational & Strategic Insights
Certain products generate high volume but low revenue impact
Underperforming categories dilute overall profitability
Data supports targeted inventory, pricing, and marketing optimization
